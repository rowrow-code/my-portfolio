Our Story

A Taste of Real Aruba

Welcome to The Pastechi House — the heart of authentic Aruban street food right in the center of Oranjestad. We're passionate about bringing you the real flavors of the island, one golden pastry at a time.

A pastechi is a traditional Aruban fried pastry — a flaky, golden shell filled with delicious savory fillings. This beloved local snack has been part of island life for generations, enjoyed for breakfast, lunch, or anytime you need a taste of home.

Paired with our handcrafted smoothies, milkshakes, and refreshing frappes, every visit to The Pastechi House is a full Caribbean experience. Come hungry, leave happy! 🌊
